Cagbalete Island Success

  June 9, 2015 - June 11, 2015

The team stayed at Joven's resort There’s no electricity on the island during day time. Charging gadgets will be available at night but permission is needed at the resort. There will be an estimated 250m walking to the resort. Sari-sari stores are available but 250m walk again is needed, be prepared. Cooking station is available at the resort.

Note:

There’s no electricity on the island during day time. Charging gadgets will be available at night but permission is needed at the resort. There will be an estimated 250m walking to the resort. Sari-sari stores are available but 250m walk again is needed, be prepared. Cooking station is available at the resort.

Need to bring:

  •   Food (Cooked or Pre-Cooked)
  •   Tent (Can share with a friend)
  •   Drinking Water (Minimum of 2liters per person)
  •   Liquor/Beer
  •   Extra Cash

Island Offers:

  •   Bar
  •   Karaoke
  •   Beach Sports (Volley Ball, Frisbee)
  •   Water Activities (Snorkelling, Kayaking, Island Hopping, Skim Boarding)

Extras Info:

  •   ₱ 250 - Rent Space for Tent /day (You can share tent with others)
  •   ₱ 1500 - Kubo (2-4pax) w/ balcony and wall fan
  •   ₱ 2000 - Kubo (6pax) w/ celling fan
  •   ₱ 2500 - Air conditioned Room (2-4 person)
  •   ₱ 3500 - P4000 - Air conditioned Rooms (6-8pax) w/ own Toilet
  •   ₱ 1200 – 1500 - Island Hopping for (8-10 person).
  •   ₱ 200 - Solo Hopping
  •   ₱ 100 - Snorkeling per hour
  •   ₱ 200 - Kayaking (Single)
  •   ₱ 300 - Kayaking (Double)
  •   ₱ 50 - Frisbie
  •   ₱ 5 - Karaoke per song
  •   ₱ 100 - Parking space per night (at Pier or South Elementary School)

Best month to travel:

  •   January to August

    • The local said that during "BER" months the island is flooding most of the week so it could be rough for the travellers. But most resort gives a 20% discount because of the rainy day season. Will you dare warrior?
