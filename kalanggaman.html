Daily Warrior - Kalanggaman Island

Heads up:

  • Book early! Kalanggaman Island is a well preserved, there’s a limit of 300 visitors per day. You need to wait for people to return to the main land. Try contacting the Palompon Tourisim for reservation, just to make sure.

    Unlike some of the resorts, Kalanggaman Island has few resources. An example is fresh water, food and electricity. You have to bring those things from the mainland. By the way, once you are oriented by the tourism guys, they will provide two garbage bags (white for the biodegradable and black for the non-biodegradable). Please clean as you go.
